Rot13

Rot13 is a substitution cipher that replaces a letter with 13 letters after it. Rot13 uses exactly half of the alphabet and therefore only two shifts are possible.

In the real world, Rot13 is used on online forums as a mean for competitions, games, hiding spoilets, puzzle solutions and hiding offensive material.
